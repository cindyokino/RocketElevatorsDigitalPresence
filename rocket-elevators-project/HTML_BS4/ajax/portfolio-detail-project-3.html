The Botanica Residence by GUZ Architects

Glass elevator

Botanica Residence is a multi-level house in Singapore with water features designed by GUZ Architects. The stunning home overlooking the Unesco World Heritage Site of the Botanic Gardens has a L-shaped swimming pool and a spiral staircase that wraps arounds a glass elevator by Rocket Elevators.

Architecture

This tropical paradise also features a waterfall surrounded by lush plants that cascades down to a natural pool below. Large cantilevers and horizontal planes make the building appear to float and photovoltaic cells cover the roof.
