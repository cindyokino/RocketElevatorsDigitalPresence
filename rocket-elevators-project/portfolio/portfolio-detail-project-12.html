Le Bourbon Condominiums

Downtown

Nestled in a vibrant and festive area of downtown along Sainte-Catherine St. East – Le Bourbon is surrounded by some of Montreal’s finest hotspots, including a restaurant and dining scene that is sure to please any foodie..

Festivals

During the summer season, Sainte-Catherine street becomes a pedestrian-only walkway, attracting enjoyable events as it becomes the hub for festivals, fireworks & nightlife. .

Attractive

New construction projects in the works will continue to make the area even more attractive. Le Bourbon is in the heart of the media neighbourhood, an area that shows amazing potential as it goes through some major regeneration projects..
