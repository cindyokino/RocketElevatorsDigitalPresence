YUL condos, downtown Montreal

800 units

Located in downtown Montreal, the YUL condo project includes two towers of 38 floors with more than 800 units (studios, one, two or three bedrooms).

Design

All common areas furniture is designed by Armani / Casa which are great in combining comfort, harmony and functionality.

Consortium

YUL Condominiums Inc. is a consortium managed by Groupe Brivia and Groupe Tianco, the Canadian division of the Chinese private group Gansu Tianqing Real Estate.
